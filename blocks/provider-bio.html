Nicholas is a bilingual (Spanish/English) Speech-Language Pathologist with experience working with both children and adults in outpatient and inpatient settings. He takes a strengths-based approach to therapy, building on each client's unique abilities to support meaningful progress. Whether he's guiding a child through the transition from tube feeds to eating by mouth, or helping an adult find their voice again after a stroke, Nicholas brings warmth, skill, and a deep belief in every client's potential.
